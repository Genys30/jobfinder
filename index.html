    About JobFinder

    JobFinder is a data intelligence platform that centralizes, standardizes, and enriches the fragmented Israeli job market. It continuously cleanses raw operational data across tech, civic tech, public sectors, and healthcare to deliver a high-signal search experience.

    ⚙️ Capabilities

    • Multi-Source Pipeline: Automated ingestion and cross-platform schema normalization.
    • Advanced Taxonomy: Granular classification by role, tier, location, and structural employer type.
    • Embedded BI Analytics: Interactive live-charts mapping hiring velocities, leading companies, and remote-work trends.
    • Productivity Tools: Saved local searches, an integrated application tracker, and CSV data exports.

    🌐 Aggregated Sources

    🚀 Tech ATS: LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Comeet, Lever, Ashby, Workable.
    🏛️ Public & Defence: Ministry of Defense (MOD), Mitam.
    🤝 Nonprofits & NGOs: Joint (JDC), Biltiformali, Impact networks.
    🎓 Academia: Weizmann, Technion, HUJI, TAU, BGU, Haifa, BAR.
    🏥 Healthcare: Clalit, Ichilov, Rambam, Hadassah, Soroka, and 10+ medical centers.
    🏢 Advisory: KPMG, Deloitte, EY, BIS.
